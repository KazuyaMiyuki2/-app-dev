# Weak Hero

## Overview

**Weak Hero** is a *South Korean* web series that tells the intense and emotional story of a seemingly fragile student who takes on school bullies using his intelligence and strategic fighting skills.

> "You don’t need strength to win — just the will and the mind."

---

### Poster

![Weak Hero Poster](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/3/3d/Weak_Hero_Class_1.jpg)

---

### Main Characters

1. **Yeon Si-eun** — A top student who uses his sharp mind to fight back against bullies.  
2. **Ahn Su-ho** — Loyal and courageous, he becomes Si-eun’s closest friend.  
3. **Oh Beom-seok** — A complex character with internal struggles.  

---

### Themes and Highlights

- **Bullying and Violence** — The harsh realities of school life.  
- **Friendship and Loyalty** — Strong bonds formed under pressure.  
- **Mental Strength** — Using intelligence as a weapon.
